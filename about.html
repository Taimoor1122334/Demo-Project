About cogic world missions

WHO WE ARE

At COGIC World Missions, our vision is to make disciples in every nation. We are committed to holistic evangelism, leadership empowerment, and raising up kingdom partners across generations. Through strategic outreach and intentional partnerships, we equip spirit-filled believers to go, serve and make a lasting impact.

Our vision

Our vision is to make disciples in every nation in the world.

Our Mission

- To lead people to Christ through holistic evangelism, training them to be spirit-filled disciples.
- To empower pastors and plant spirit-filled churches.
- To recruit diverse and lasting kingdom partners, of all generations, who will invest resources in the vision.

We will do this through strategic platforms to promote Missions awareness.

Discipleship

Our COGIC World Missions newsletter provides updates on outreach mission opportunities. Submit your name and email address to receive our monthly newsletter.

COGIC World Missions exists to fulfill Christ's command to make disciples of all nations. Actively serving in 113 countries under Presiding Bishop J. Drew Sheard, the ministry encourages leaders at every level of the Church of God in Christ to renew their focus on global outreach. By addressing both spiritual growth and real-world challenges, the mission aims to deepen the church's global impact.
