Why Give to Missions?

Giving to missions is more than a financial transaction—it’s an act of global discipleship, a step toward healing hearts and communities across borders.

Every donation advances our commitment to share the Gospel, serve those in need, and respond with compassion to both spiritual and physical realities. From providing meals to children and shelter to families, to empowering pastors and missionaries with the tools to teach and lead—we steward each gift with care, so that lives are changed in every nation we reach.

Your generosity fuels discipleship, multiplies service, and meets people where they are—with dignity, hope, and love.

Strategic Funding Areas

COGIC World Missions is committed to global impact through targeted funding. Each priority reflects a vital area of support:


Grants and Stipends

Strengthening churches, schools, clinics, orphanages, and community development projects through financial support for international (non-US) initiatives. Jurisdictional Bishops, Supervisors and Missions Directors are eligible to apply for these funds for their Jurisdictional projects and initiatives.


Regional Pastors Conferences

Facilitating training sessions with pastors – providing a regional venue, speaker support, and learning materials to encourage cross-cultural leadership development to further church planting and church growth.


International Delegates

Supporting the participation of international delegates to Holy Convocation through lodging, registration, and transportation. This support fosters global fellowship, cross-cultural exchange, and shared vision among mission leaders and partners around the world


International Emergency Relief

Providing rapid financial response in times of crisis—natural disasters, political unrest, or urgent medical needs in partnership with COGIC Charities.


Resident Missionaries

Funding housing, transportation, and local resources for missionaries serving abroad long-term. This not only sustains their day-to-day responsibilities but supports their ability to live and serve effectively in the field.


Operational Expenses

Ensuring the sustainability of daily operations that keep our missions work effective and accountable, including global site visits, marketing and tools to raise awareness of the exciting achievements of COGIC World Missions.

COGIC World Missions exists to fulfill Christ's command to make disciples of all nations. Actively serving in 113 countries under Presiding Bishop J. Drew Sheard, the ministry encourages leaders at every level of the Church of God in Christ to renew their focus on global outreach. By addressing both spiritual growth and real-world challenges, the mission aims to deepen the church's global impact.
